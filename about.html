History

Raymar Adventures is a tours and travel agency founded in February 2020. The founders of the agensy are passionate travelers who spend most of their time touring the world. They were inspired to make travel and tour hassle-free and bring every traveler a step closer to their next dream destination. The founders, both computer programmers, are school colleagues who came up with the idea for a travel and adventure website which started off as a review website.

Mission

Fulfilling life one adventure at a time.

We live through experiences, they make our lives exciting. Our company aims to improve the quality of people's lives through creating and providing memorable touring experiences for them.

Vision

To become Kenya's most affordable touring agency while providing our clients with the best adventures without hurting their pockets. We aim to increasingly provide quality services by taking the feedback we receive in to consideration. Our goal is to tailor experiences for our clients to fit their specific needs.
