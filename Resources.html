Kambi Amani, Nakuru
KES 6,000 per Night

Featuring a sun terrace with panoramic views of Lake Nakuru, Nakuru town, local mountain ranges and surrounding villages, Kambi Amaniis set in Nakuru. Free private parking is also available at this tented camp. Bed linen is featured. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant. The property also offers packed lunches. Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for bird-watching. Hyrax Hill Museum is 26.9 km away. Lake Naivasha, Hell's Gate National Park, Thompson Falls and lake Baringo are attractions that can be visited from Kambi Amani.

Book at: Kambi amani.

Vienna Woods Holiday Inn Hotel
KES 4,800 per Night

Set in Nakuru, Vienna Woods Holiday Inn Hotel has a garden and bar. Located around 16 km from Egerton Castle, the hotel with free WiFi is also 2 km away from Westside Mall. The property is 10 km from Lake Nakuru National Park.At the hotel a garden is available, each room has a terrace. Each room comes with a private bathroom. The daily breakfast offers continental and American options. A well stocked bar is also available.

Book at: Vienna Woods.

Brooks Lodge
KES 5,500 per Night

Located In Gilgil, 5 km from town and 2 km from Gilgil Polo club and Pembroke International school. Brooks Lodge provides free WiFi and mountain views. Fitted with a patio, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bath and free toiletries. Some units also have a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher, a microwave, and a toaster. A continental, buffet or à la carte breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves international cuisine. The lodge offers a children's playground. Guests can relax in the garden at the property. Lake Nakuru National Park is 12 km from Brooks Lodge, while Egerton Castle is 15 km from the property.
